Creating and Sending Invoices

Creating invoices in Fintle is quick and simple, allowing you to get paid faster and keep your finances organized.

Step-by-Step Guide

1Log in to your Fintle account

Navigate to Invoicing from the main menu.

2Create New Invoice

Click on Create New Invoice to start the process.

3Add Client Information

Choose an existing client from your list or add a new one by entering their name, email, and business details.

4Fill Out Invoice Details

  • Invoice Number (automatically generated, but you can edit if needed)
  • Issue Date and Due Date
  • Payment Terms (Net 7, Net 30, etc.)

5Add Items or Services

Enter descriptions, quantity, unit price, and applicable taxes.

6Review the Total

Fintle automatically calculates the subtotal, taxes, and final amount.

7Add Notes or Terms

Optional section for payment instructions, thank-you notes, or legal terms.

8Save & Preview

Review your invoice before sending.

9Send Invoice

Click Send Invoice to deliver it directly to your client's email or share the link.

Key Features

  • Send invoices instantly via email.
  • Real-time notifications when clients view or pay invoices.
  • Multiple payment options supported (bank transfer, credit card, etc.).
  • Track invoice status: Draft, Sent, Viewed, Paid, or Overdue.
