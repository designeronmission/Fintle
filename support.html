Creating and Sending Invoices

Creating invoices in Fintle is quick and simple, allowing you to get paid faster and keep your finances organized.

Step-by-Step Guide

1Log in to your Fintle account

Access your dashboard using your credentials. If you don't have an account yet, you can sign up for free.

2Navigate to the Invoices section

Click on the "Invoices" tab in the main navigation menu, then select "Create New Invoice".

3Fill in client details

Select an existing client from your database or add a new client by entering their name, address, and contact information.

4Add invoice items

List all products or services with descriptions, quantities, rates, and applicable taxes. You can add multiple line items as needed.

5Set payment terms

Specify payment due date, accepted payment methods, and any early payment discounts or late payment fees.

6Review and send

Preview your invoice to ensure all information is correct, then send it directly to your client via email or download it as a PDF.

Pro Tip: Save time by using invoice templates for recurring services or common projects.

Setting Up Recurring Invoices

Automate your billing process with Fintle's recurring invoices feature, perfect for subscription-based services or retainers.

How to Create Recurring Invoices

1Create a new invoice

Follow the standard invoice creation process as outlined above.

2Enable recurrence

Toggle the "Recurring" option and set your preferred frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, or annually).

3Set start and end dates

Specify when the recurring invoices should begin and if they should automatically stop after a certain number of occurrences or on a specific date.

4Save as template

Save your settings as a template for future use or to create similar recurring invoices quickly.

Benefit: Recurring invoices are automatically generated and sent according to your schedule, saving you time and ensuring consistent cash flow.

Managing Recurring Invoices

  • View all active recurring invoices from the "Recurring" tab in the Invoices section
  • Pause, edit, or cancel recurring invoices at any time
  • Review history of sent invoices from each recurring series
  • Receive notifications before invoices are automatically sent

Setting Up Tax Rates

Configure tax settings to ensure accurate calculations and compliance with local regulations.

Adding Tax Rates

1Access Tax Settings

Navigate to Settings > Taxes from your main dashboard.

2Create New Tax Rate

Click "Add Tax Rate" and enter the tax name (e.g., VAT, GST, Sales Tax) and percentage.

3Configure Tax Settings

Specify whether the tax is inclusive or exclusive, and if it applies to all items or specific ones.

4Set Default Tax Rates

Designate default tax rates for different types of products or services.

Tax Types Supported

Fintle supports various tax structures to accommodate businesses worldwide:

GST VAT Sales Tax Import Duty Excise Tax
Important: Consult with a tax professional to ensure you're applying the correct tax rates for your location and business type.

Generating Tax Reports

Fintle provides comprehensive tax reporting features to simplify your compliance process.

Available Tax Reports

Sales Tax Summary

Overview of collected sales tax by period, jurisdiction, and tax rate.

VAT/GST Report

Detailed breakdown of VAT/GST collected on sales and paid on purchases.

Taxable vs. Non-Taxable Sales

Segmentation of your revenue by taxable status for accurate reporting.

Export Report

Summary of international sales for VAT/GST purposes.

How to Generate Reports

1Go to Reports Section

Navigate to Reports > Tax Reports from your main menu.

2Select Report Type

Choose the specific tax report you need from the available options.

3Set Date Range

Specify the period for which you need the tax information.

4Generate and Export

Generate the report and export it as PDF, Excel, or CSV for your records or to share with your accountant.

GST/VAT Compliance

Stay compliant with GST and VAT regulations using Fintle's dedicated features.

GST Features

GSTIN Tracking

Store and display GST identification numbers for your business and clients.

HSN/SAC Codes

Add HSN (Harmonized System of Nomenclature) or SAC (Services Accounting Code) to invoice items.

GSTR Report Preparation

Generate reports formatted specifically for GSTR filing requirements.

VAT Features

EU VAT Compliance

Handle EU VAT rules including reverse charge mechanism for B2B transactions.

VAT MOSS

Support for VAT MOSS (Mini One Stop Shop) for digital services across EU.

VAT Number Validation

Verify EU VAT numbers directly within Fintle to ensure accuracy.

Note: Tax laws and regulations change frequently. While Fintle provides tools to help with compliance, we recommend consulting with a tax professional for specific advice related to your business.
