Creating and Sending Invoices

Creating invoices in Fintle is quick and simple, allowing you to get paid faster and keep your finances organized.

Step-by-Step Guide

1Log in to your Fintle account

Navigate to Invoicing from the main menu.

2Create New Invoice

Click on Create New Invoice to start the process.

3Add Client Information

Choose an existing client from your list or add a new one by entering their name, email, and business details.

4Fill Out Invoice Details
  • Invoice Number (automatically generated, but you can edit if needed)
  • Issue Date and Due Date
  • Payment Terms (Net 7, Net 30, etc.)
5Add Items or Services

Enter descriptions, quantity, unit price, and applicable taxes.

6Review the Total

Fintle automatically calculates the subtotal, taxes, and final amount.

7Add Notes or Terms

Optional section for payment instructions, thank-you notes, or legal terms.

8Save & Preview

Review your invoice before sending.

9Send Invoice

Click Send Invoice to deliver it directly to your client's email or share the link.

Key Features

  • Send invoices instantly via email.
  • Real-time notifications when clients view or pay invoices.
  • Multiple payment options supported (bank transfer, credit card, etc.).
  • Track invoice status: Draft, Sent, Viewed, Paid, or Overdue.

Setting Up Recurring Invoices

If you work with subscription clients or provide regular services, Recurring Invoices help automate your billing so you never miss a payment.

How to Set Up a Recurring Invoice

1Navigate to Recurring Invoices

Go to Invoicing > Recurring Invoices.

2Create New Recurring Invoice

Click New Recurring Invoice to start the setup process.

3Choose Client

Select the client you want to bill automatically.

4Set Frequency

Options include weekly, monthly, quarterly, or custom. Choose the start date and the number of occurrences (or make it indefinite).

5Add Invoice Details

Specify services, products, tax rates, and payment terms.

6Automation Settings
  • Enable automatic sending (invoice sent to client without manual action)
  • Enable automatic payment collection (if the client has a saved payment method)
7Review & Activate

Once confirmed, invoices will be generated and sent automatically.

Benefits

  • Save time by eliminating repetitive manual invoicing.
  • Ensure consistent cash flow with on-time billing.
  • Reduce missed or delayed payments.
  • Fully customizable to match each client's billing cycle.

Customizing Invoice Templates

Your invoices represent your business, and with Fintle's Invoice Template Customization, you can make them look professional and on-brand.

How to Customize Your Template

1Access Template Settings

Go to Settings > Invoice Templates.

2Choose a Template

Select a default template or create a new one.

3Personalize Layout & Style
  • Add your company logo and brand colors
  • Select fonts, styles, and header/footer layout
  • Adjust spacing and positioning of key elements
4Configure Invoice Fields
  • Show/hide client details, tax ID, or purchase order number
  • Add custom fields such as project reference or discount codes
5Set Default Messages

Add payment instructions, bank account details, or thank-you notes.

6Save Your Template

Set it as default for all invoices, or apply it only to selected clients.

Customization Options

  • Brand Identity: Add your logo, tagline, and color scheme.
  • Flexible Layouts: Choose from multiple styles (classic, modern, compact).
  • Legal & Compliance: Include tax registration numbers, digital signatures, or required disclaimers.
  • Multi-Language & Multi-Currency support for global clients.

Why Customize?

  • Strengthen your brand identity with every invoice.
  • Improve client trust with professional presentation.
  • Ensure compliance with industry and tax regulations.
